How to use

Curves

Curve Presets

• You have 5 preset curves and 20 custom slots.

• Click on the + to add new curves.

• Right click existing curves to edit or remove them.

• If you Left click while you have keyframes selected, you can apply the current curve.

Instant Curve

• This tab can be accessed by just rightclicking the curve navigation icon.

• Make your curve and apply it directly to keyframes.

Main

• Speed: Changes the Speed of the selected Layer.

• Transform: Changes Rotation, Flip side or or the origin in the Timeline of the selected Layer.

• Scale to comp: Sets the scale of the selected clips to the Composition.

• Frameblending: Turns Frameblending on/off for the whole Project.

• Guide Lines: Tap it once to add a crosshair at the center of your Preview. Tap twice to overlay a grid. Pressing it a third time removes the overlay. Note: Ensure the 'guidelines' option is enabled under 'views' to make them visible.

• Null, Camera, Text, Adjustment Layer, Solid: Adds a precut (Null, Camera, Text, Adjustment Layer, Solid) over the selected Layer or if no layer is selected on top of the Composition

• Pre-compose: Pre-composes all selected clips.

Effects

• Hover over a button to see a preview of the Effect/Shake on the right panel.

• Click on the little "gear" Icon to open the Settings tab for the current Effect you're on.

• For the shake presets you can adjust the settings right on the bottom where it says "Settings".

• Use the "in" button to start the transition and the "out" button to end the transition

• Just put ur assets in the "\Documents\JerryFlow\Assets" folder and reload. You can also click the text on the bottom left to open the folder. (Open Folder)

• With left click you can add the asset into ur timeline. With a right click you can add or remove it as favourite.

• To navigate just scroll with your mouse. Scroll down goes right, scroll up goes left

Settings

• Set the color of your layers that JerryFlow creates

• You can change the hue of the whole UI or set it to RGB if you want.

• If you find the Icon-animations a bit to distracting, you can Disable them.

• Customize the UI Animation speed from 0ms to 300ms.
